Pepper Sea Club is different - a place where quiet luxury meets nature, a place of serenity and well-being with luxurious amenities designed in contemporary bohemian style. Pepper Sea Club comprises 72 luxurious guest rooms and 23 suites, many with their own pool. Interiors encapsulate Pepper’s ‘free spirit’ with furnishings and fittings reflecting Pepper’s underlying design philosophy - a celebration of warm natural materials, artisanal crafts and traditional craftsmanship. King-size beds with luxury mattresses and high-quality bedlinen, along with stunning state-of-the-art bathrooms complete the picture.

GASTRONOMY

A PASSION FOR AUTHENTICITY

Pepper Sea Club is for wanderers with a passion for authenticity, the taste of nature’s harvest and life-enhancing gastronomy. Pepper’s inspired and regularly changing menus offer a diverse range of rich and healthy culinary experiences - from international favourites to traditional Greek and Cretan cuisine. Inspired by the life-enhancing ingredients of the Cretan diet, Pepper’s gastronomic offerings are found in every restaurant and bar.
